Please note that available payment options may vary based on your location.

  • Payments

  • Can I have an invoice for my subscription?

    Certainly! We provide an electronic invoice for all subscription payments. Once your payment is processed, you will receive an email with the invoice attached, or you can access it directly from your account dashboard. If you need any additional information or assistance regarding your invoice, feel free to reach out to our customer support team.

  • Why did my credit card or PayPal payment fail?

    There are several reasons why a payment might fail. Common reasons include insufficient funds, incorrect card details, expired card, or an issue with the PayPal account. Please double-check your payment information for accuracy and ensure sufficient funds are available. If the problem persists, you may want to contact your bank or PayPal customer support to resolve any potential issues on their end. Alternatively, you can try using a different payment method to complete your purchase. If you still encounter difficulties, don't hesitate to contact our support team for further assistance. We are here to help!

  • Why does my bank statement show multiple charges for one upgrade?

    The multiple charges on your bank statement are likely temporary authorizations by your bank to verify your payment method during the upgrade process. Rest assured, only one actual charge for the upgrade will be processed, and any additional holds will be automatically released by your bank within a few days. If you encounter any issues or need further assistance, please contact our support team for prompt resolution.

Sometimes, there might be unforeseen delays in transit. If there are no updates or you have concerns, reach out to our customer support team with your order details, and we'll investigate the matter promptly. Your satisfaction is important to us, and we'll work diligently to ensure your order reaches you as soon as possible.
